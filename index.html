4. Permissions Explained

Cat App requests the following permissions solely for its core functionality:

SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW

Allows the app to display characters as overlay elements on top of other apps. This permission is essential for showing the cute characters in your status bar area.

FOREGROUND_SERVICE & FOREGROUND_SERVICE_SPECIAL_USE

Enables the app to run continuously in the background to maintain the display of characters. This ensures the characters remain visible even when you're using other apps.

POST_NOTIFICATIONS

Allows the app to show notifications related to the foreground service status. This is required by Android for apps running background services and helps you manage the app's operation.

Important: These permissions are used exclusively for the app's intended functionality and not for any data collection or tracking purposes.
